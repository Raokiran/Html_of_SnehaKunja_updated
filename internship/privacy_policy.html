5.1 You can choose not to provide certain personal information, but this may limit your access to certain features of the website.
5.2 You may opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the instructions provided in such communications.

6.1 Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these sites.
7. Children's Privacy
7.1 Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that we have unintentionally collected information from a child, please contact us to remove the information.
8. Changes to this Privacy Policy
8.1 We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices and the law. We will notify users of any significant changes via email or by prominently posting a notice on our website.
9. Contact Information
